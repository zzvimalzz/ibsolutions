About Us

Built on real-world experience, driven by meaningful outcomes.

Our Story

Bridging Experience With Meaningful Learning

Integrated Biz Solutions (M) Sdn Bhd is a Malaysian professional services company specialising in corporate training and capability development. We focus on structured, practical, results-driven programmes that strengthen workplace competencies and support organisational growth.

Our emphasis is on real-world skill application, learner engagement, and measurable outcomes across leadership, communication, productivity, and soft skills development.

We work with a network of experienced trainers to serve SMEs, corporations, educational institutions, and public sector organisations. While professional training remains our core, we plan to expand gradually into emceeing services and selected F&B ventures — a phased development strategy to ensure steady growth while maintaining a strong L&D foundation.

"Committed to professionalism, client satisfaction, and continuous improvement."
